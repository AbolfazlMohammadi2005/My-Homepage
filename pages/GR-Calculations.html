GR-Calculations

About This Project

This is a research-oriented project in the fields of General Relativity and Cosmology. Its objective is to develop a comprehensive computational framework for numerical relativity, ranging from black hole simulations to tensor and metric calculations. The project aims to bridge theoretical foundations with practical implementations in gravitational physics. You are warmly invited to collaborate and contribute to the development of this project.
